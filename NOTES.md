# AgenticB2B — Notes

## What We Are

We build AI agents for industrial SMBs in India.

A services agency, not a product company. We come in, understand your operations, and build the automation layer that ties it together.

---

## Who We Serve

Small to medium industrial businesses in India — manufacturing, physical products, B2B sales.
Typically 10–200 employees. Operations run on WhatsApp, email, and memory. Little to no digital infrastructure.

---

## The Problem We Solve

These businesses run across 4–5 tools that don't talk to each other. Data lives in Excel, WhatsApp, and someone's memory.

Specifically:
- Data scattered across tools, never reconciled
- Too many disconnected software — no single view of the business
- ERP either missing, broken, or mid-migration
- Third-party tools that almost fit but not quite — no custom features

---

## What We Offer

We come in, understand your operations, and build the automation layer that ties it together.

**Track 1 — Revenue Operations (beachhead)**
Leads arrive from exhibitions, WhatsApp, email, phone, distributors — all scattered. No pipeline visibility, no follow-up system. Founders lose orders they don't even know about.
- AI layer on top of existing tools (WhatsApp, email) — not a replacement CRM
- Structures incoming leads automatically
- Automates follow-up sequences
- Surfaces hot leads with context

**Track 2 — Process Intelligence (exploratory)**
Industrial processes generate data nobody is using. Decisions are made on intuition, not signal.
- Purpose-built agents for specific industrial process problems
- Modelling, prediction, automation at the process level
- Needs further customer discovery before we go wide

---

## Beachhead Strategy

- Design partner: Dynemech (anti-vibration mounts) — validating Track 1 first
- Geographic focus: one industrial cluster (Pune / Rajkot / Coimbatore)
- Distribution: cofounder network as primary go-to-market
- Goal: 3–5 design partners, learn fast, then expand
