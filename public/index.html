§01 — The problem

Software was sold. Operations were not solved.

We've sat with founders running ₹50 Cr to ₹500 Cr industrial businesses. The pattern is the same — tools layered over tools, none of them talking, and someone in accounts holding it all together with a spreadsheet.

01 →

Disconnected tools

Your business runs across 4–5 software that don't talk to each other. No single view of what's actually happening on the floor.

02 →

Data never reconciled

Numbers in Excel don't match the ERP. Someone manually copies data between systems every single day, and errors compound.

03 →

An ERP that doesn't fit

Either you don't have one, you're mid-migration, or it works for 80% of your operations and breaks on the 20% that matter most.

04 →

No room for custom

Third-party tools almost fit your workflow. But the one feature you actually need? It isn't there, and nobody will build it.

§02 — Our approach

We don't sell you another piece of software. We make the ones you have actually work.

01 —

Map first, build second

We spend the first weeks shadowing your team. We don't propose anything until we understand exactly how a purchase order moves from WhatsApp to dispatch.

02 —

Work with what's there

Tally, SAP, Zoho, Excel, WhatsApp — we plug into your existing stack. No rip-and-replace. No three-month migration.

03 —

Outcomes, not dashboards

We measure success by hours saved, errors avoided, and decisions made faster — not by how pretty the dashboard looks.
