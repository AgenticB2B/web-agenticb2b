§01 The problem

Your business can't run without you in the room.

It's not just you. The person in accounts who knows which cheque is where. The salesperson whose WhatsApp is your only deal record. The floor manager who carries three years of complaints in his head. Remove any one, and parts of your business go with them.

01

The data you actually need isn't captured anywhere

How long does an order take from receipt to dispatch? Which vendor is late 40% of the time? Which five customers make up 80% of your revenue? Your data is scattered across 4–5 systems that don't talk to each other.

02

Your customers chase you. Your vendors chase you.

Your customers are begging to tell you what's wrong. They do it by calling and giving two abuses, or two compliments, to whoever picks up. Customers call asking "where's my order?". Vendors follow up on payments that are late. Every communication is manual, reactive, and late.

03

The vendor you trusted now owns you

You commissioned custom software. Today the vendor is the only person who understands the codebase — and they're raising prices every year. Off-the-shelf tools almost fit, but the one feature you actually need isn't there, and nobody will build it. You can't leave. You can't stay. You pay.

04

You're becoming invisible to the buyers who matter most

Large enterprises are already using procurement agents. Your pricing lives in PDF quotations. Your lead times are "we'll let you know." Your RFQ responses happen on email. You are becoming invisible to the buyers you need most — and you will never see the opportunities you're missing.

05

Intelligence evaporates the moment it's created

You hired the smart operations head. You pay them well. Every decision they make, every correction they apply, every vendor they fix — happens in a conversation, and vapourises the second it ends. Over five years, that's crores of rupees of thinking that left no trace.

06

Your team is already using AI. You just don't know what they're pasting.

Your people already use free ChatGPT, free Gemini, free Perplexity. They paste customer data, contracts, vendor negotiations, financials — into tools designed to train on those inputs. You don't know what's leaving. You couldn't stop it if you wanted to, because you've given them nothing safer.

§02 Our approach

We don't sell you another piece of software. We make the ones you have actually work.

01

Map first, build second

We spend the first weeks shadowing your team. We don't propose anything until we understand exactly how a purchase order moves from WhatsApp to dispatch.

02

Work with what's there

Tally, SAP, Zoho, Excel, WhatsApp. We plug into your existing stack. No rip-and-replace. No three-month migration.

03

Outcomes, not dashboards

We measure success by hours saved, errors avoided, and decisions made faster, not by how pretty the dashboard looks.
